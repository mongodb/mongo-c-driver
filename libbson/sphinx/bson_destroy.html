bson_destroy()¶

Synopsis¶

void
bson_destroy (bson_t *bson);

Parameters¶

  • bson: A bson_t.

Description¶

The bson_destroy() function shall free an allocated bson_t structure.

This function should always be called when you are done with a bson_t unless otherwise specified.
